We are a team of highly motivated Bachelor, Master and PhD students (and a few Post-Docs...) interested in advancing the fields of autonomous control.
To test our approaches we use the [RoboRacer](https://roboracer.ai/) platform, a racing platform for 1:10 scale autonomous cars.

For more information about our team, refer to [our webpage](https://scuderia-segfault.at/).
